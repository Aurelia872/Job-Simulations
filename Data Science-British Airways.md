## This is a job simulation to uncover customer insights and predict customer behavior with the British Airways Data Science team.
### Source : [Forage](https://www.theforage.com/simulations/british-airways/data-science-yqoz)
***
### Project outcomes
<ul>
  <li>Understand how using airline data and modeling helps British Airways forecast lounge demand and plan for future capacity planning </li>
  <li>Understand how using data and predictive models helps British Airways acquire customers before they embark on their holidays </li>
</ul>

***
### Tasks Overview
<p><h2>Task 01  : Modeling lounge eligibility at Heathrow Terminal 3 </h2></p>
<ol>
  <li> Review lounge eligibility criteria and explore how customer groupings can inform lounge demand assumptions </li>
  <li> Create a reusable lookup table and written justification that British Airways can apply to future flying schedules </li>
</ol>
<p><h2>Task 02  : Predicting customer buying behaviour</h2> </p>
<ol>
  <li> Prepare a dataset </li>
  <li> Train a machine learning model </li>
  <li> Evaluate and present findings </li>
</ol>
